          ABOUT

          TrackNStudy is a productivity platform designed to help students organize, manage, and elevate their study experience. Whether you're preparing for exams, tracking assignments, or simply trying to stay focused, this website brings together essential tools in one clean, accessible space.

          Built with simplicity and functionality in mind, TrackNStudy empowers users to take control of their academic journey. Each feature is crafted to reduce stress, boost efficiency, and make studying feel less overwhelming and more rewarding.

          From note-taking to time management, this platform is your all-in-one study companion — perfect for learners who value structure, clarity, and a touch of creativity.

          This website was proudly created by a Grade 12 student from STI and completed in 2025.

          FEATURES

          Here's a breakdown of all the tools available on TrackNStudy — each one designed to support a specific part of your study workflow:

          • Main Notes – A clean space to write, organize, and revisit your key study notes.
          • To-Do List – Stay on top of your tasks with a simple checklist system.
          • Assignment Tracker – Log your upcoming assignments with due dates and descriptions.
          • Study Timer – Use focused time blocks (like Pomodoro) to stay productive.
          • Calculator – A built-in scientific calculator for quick math operations and advanced functions.
          • Sticky Notes – Jot down reminders, ideas, or motivational quotes.
          • Calendar – View your schedule at a glance.
          • Reviewer – Upload or create review materials for quizzes and exams.
          • Converter – Convert units, currencies, or values with ease.
          • Games – Take short breaks with educational or relaxing games.
          • Periodic Table – Explore elements interactively.
