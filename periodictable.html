How to Explore the Periodic Table

Welcome to the Interactive Periodic Table—a dynamic tool designed to make chemistry intuitive, engaging, and beautiful.

  • 🔍 Use the search bar to instantly find any element by name or symbol.
  • 🎨 Filter by category or phase to explore patterns and properties.
  • 🧠 Click any element to reveal its details in the info panel.
  • 🎛️ Switch between visual themes to suit your mood or learning style.
  • ⌨️ Navigate with your keyboard for a faster experience.

Whether you're a student, a teacher, or just curious about the building blocks of matter, this table is built to spark discovery. Dive in, experiment, and let your curiosity lead the way.
